About

Protection you can verify.

Self-healing films and ceramic coatings, with a tamper-evident digital warranty for every install.

What we do

Every roll of X-PPF film carries a unique ID. Authorised studios scan that roll into their inventory, then scan it again when they wrap a client's vehicle. The platform matches the two automatically, deducts the roll from stock, and issues a warranty certificate — co-branded with either the studio's identity or X-PPF's. No paperwork, no guesswork.

Why it matters

A warranty is only worth what backs it. Tying each certificate to a verified, single-use roll means a client can trust the coverage is genuine, and a studio can prove the film on the car is authentic X-PPF.
